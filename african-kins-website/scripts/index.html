About Us

African Kings Suppliers Limited was incorporated under the Companies Act (Cap.486) on December 9th, 2011, as a Private Limited Company dealing in general supplies. In April 2020, we transitioned into medical supplies, focusing on hospital and laboratory equipment, reagents, and consumables.

Our goal is to offer the best value for money while ensuring the highest quality standards for our clients. We are a young, energetic, and dedicated team, committed to transparency, honesty, and excellence in our services.

We offer a variety of known quality brands including: Coming, Nantong, iCubio, Biobase, Evergreen, Labcare, Shubham, Diaspot among others.
